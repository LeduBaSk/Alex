About Me

Passionate English Educator & Child Development Specialist

Hello! I'm Crystal Ann Vince, an experienced English teacher specializing in teaching children aged 5-15, providing personalized lessons using interactive materials such as educational videos and games. With over 8 years of teaching experience at PandaSchool Group, I've helped hundreds of students develop their English skills while nurturing their confidence and love for learning.

My teaching philosophy centers on creating a warm, supportive environment where children feel safe to express themselves. I believe every child has unique strengths and learning styles, and my approach focuses on discovering and building upon these individual qualities.

I hold a BE in English Culture and Literature from Perdue University and Master Degree from Charles University in Prague, also I am a methodologist. I am TEFL, TEYL, TESOL and IELTS certified, with certification to additionally teach Science and make entertaining and funny learning environment for every child.

My Teaching Approach

Interactive Learning

Engaging games and activities that make learning English fun and memorable. I use storytelling, songs, and role-playing to create immersive language experiences.

Personalized Instruction

Customized lesson plans based on each student's interests, learning style, and goals. I adapt my teaching methods to ensure every child can succeed.

Confidence Building

Creating a positive learning environment where children feel encouraged to speak, ask questions, and take risks without fear of judgment.

Cultural Connection

Introducing students to English-speaking cultures through literature, music, and traditions, helping them understand the context behind the language.

Academic Preparation

Helping students prepare for school exams and standardized tests like KET, PET, and TOEFL Junior with targeted practice and strategies.

Flexible Scheduling

Convenient class times that work around your family's schedule, with options for both regular weekly sessions and occasional one-time lessons.
